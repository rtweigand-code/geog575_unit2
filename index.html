Big Ten Athletic Revenue, 2015–2024

This map shows total athletic department revenue for 16 public Big Ten universities from 2015–2024. Use the time slider to compare how revenue differs between programs and how those differences change over time.

Look for smaller shifts that line up with major moments like COVID-19 (2020), the start of NIL (2021), and the new Big Ten media deal (2023–2024). As you move through the years, some programs begin to separate slightly, suggesting factors like donor support, alumni base, and market size may influence who gains an edge.

Data source: Knight-Newhouse College Athletics Database. Basemap: CARTO / OpenStreetMap.
