Big Ten Athletic Revenue, 2015–2024

This map shows total athletic department revenue for 16 public Big Ten universities from 2015–2024. By adjusting the time slider, users can compare how revenue levels vary between programs and how those differences have shifted during a period of major changes in college athletics, including new media rights deals, conference realignment, and the NIL era.

Data source: Knight-Newhouse College Athletics Database. Basemap: CARTO / OpenStreetMap.
